Description of various models
-----------------------------

piping.schema
piping.grammar
piping.stencil
Piping and instrumentation DSL. Stencil is the graphical editor and grammar is the textual editor.

piping-sim.schema
piping-sim.stencil
Data model and stencil code for containing and rendering simulator. Piping-sim.schema is a composition of the piping diagram (piping.schema) and its dynamic state (piping-state.schema)

controller.schema
controller.grammar
controller.stencil
Controller DSL. Has both graphical and textual editors
